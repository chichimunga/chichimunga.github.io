A collision shape defined by a custom polygon drawn by hand. Use when predefined shapes (rect, circle, capsule) aren't a close enough fit.

Alternative to [[CollisionShape2D\|CollisionShape2D]], which uses predefined shapes configured in the Inspector.

## Usage

After adding the node, click the **"Create Points"** icon in the upper-middle toolbar to begin drawing the polygon.
