## Usage

Add `Camera2D` as a child of the node it should follow (typically the player).

**Inspector settings:**
- **Position Smoothing** — enable for smooth camera movement
- **Zoom** — lower values zoom out (e.g. `0.6` shows more of the scene than `1`)
