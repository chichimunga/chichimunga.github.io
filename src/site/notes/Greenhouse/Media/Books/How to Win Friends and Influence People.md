- Do not criticize, condemn, or complain publicly
- People won't listen for long if my only communications are my latest problem, gripe, etc.
- Calm myself before talking to someone
- Speak highly of others
- Take interests in others' interests
- Smile!
- Call people by their name
- Apologize and tell the truth
- Don't take credit when things go well, don't give blame when things go wrong
- Begin conversations in a friendly manner even if I'm upset
- Begin conversations with honest and genuine appreciation
- When criticizing, do not use the word "but." Instead, use "and" or simply pause for a moment before continuing
- Make praise public and specific
